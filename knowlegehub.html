Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about systematic growth and the GLIDE Framework.

What is the GLIDE Framework?

GLIDE is our proprietary framework for measuring and building systematic business growth. It stands for Growth, Leadership, Infrastructure, Demand, and Expansion—the five core pillars every ambitious business needs to scale sustainably across Africa.

How does the free GLIDE assessment work?

The assessment takes 10-15 minutes and evaluates your business across all five GLIDE pillars. You'll receive an immediate score (0-100) with specific gaps identified and clear next steps for improvement. It's completely free with no credit card required.

What makes your approach different from other agencies?

Most agencies just execute tactics. We build systematic infrastructure. Think of it like this: tactics are renting, systems are owning. We measure everything through the GLIDE Framework, focus on compounding returns, and combine strategic thinking with hands-on execution.

How long does it take to see results?

Quick wins in 30-60 days (improved messaging, better content, clearer positioning). Systematic infrastructure takes 90-180 days to build. Compounding returns show up in months 6-12. We're building for the long game, not quick fixes that expire.

Do you work with businesses outside Botswana?

Absolutely. We're based in Gaborone but serve businesses across Africa—South Africa, Nigeria, Kenya, Ghana, Zimbabwe, and beyond. Everything is done remotely with regular video calls and strategic check-ins.

What's the investment to work with you?

Three packages: Foundation (P6,000/month), Momentum (P10,000/month), and Dominance (P15,000/month). All include the GLIDE Framework, strategic planning, and hands-on execution. We also offer one-time projects and quick wins starting at P500.

What industries do you specialize in?

We work with ambitious B2B and B2C businesses: professional services, tech companies, manufacturing, e-commerce, financial services, and hospitality. Our ideal client has P500K+ annual revenue and is ready to build systematic infrastructure.

How does AfCFTA impact my business?

AfCFTA (African Continental Free Trade Area) creates the world's largest free trade area. It reduces tariffs, simplifies cross-border trade, and opens markets across 54 countries. We help you navigate compliance, identify opportunities, and execute market entry strategies.
